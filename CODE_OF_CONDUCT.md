# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project exists to help people improve their lives through
identity-based habits, privacy, and personal ownership.

We are committed to fostering an open, respectful, and constructive
environment for everyone who participates --- regardless of experience
level, background, or identity.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Expected Behavior

By participating in this project, you agree to:

-   Be respectful and considerate in communication
-   Assume good intent
-   Offer constructive feedback
-   Stay on topic in discussions and issues
-   Respect the privacy and boundaries of others

Disagreements are natural. Handle them professionally and calmly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior will not be tolerated:

-   Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
-   Personal attacks or insults
-   Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
-   Doxxing or sharing private information
-   Persistent off-topic arguments or hostility

This includes behavior in issues, pull requests, discussions, and other
community spaces.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Privacy & Integrity

Given the nature of this project:

-   Do not request user data from others.
-   Do not introduce tracking, analytics, or dark patterns in
    contributions.
-   Respect the privacy-first principles outlined in the repository.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to:

-   Remove or edit comments
-   Close issues or pull requests
-   Block or ban contributors who violate this Code of Conduct

Enforcement decisions will aim to be fair, proportional, and aligned
with maintaining a healthy community.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of
Conduct, please report it privately:

josh@topswagcode.com

All reports will be reviewed confidentially.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

This Code of Conduct exists to protect the integrity of the project and
the well-being of its community.
